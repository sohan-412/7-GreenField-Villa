Explore Nearby Attractions

Your stay at 7 Green field Villa places you near some of the region's most beloved spots.

Mahabaleshwar

Famous for its stunning viewpoints, lush strawberry farms, and beautiful lakes. A perfect hill station day trip.

~ 2.5 Hours Drive

Sinhagad Fort

A historic hill fortress offering panoramic views, trekking opportunities, and a glimpse into Maratha history. Don't miss trying the traditional local snacks available at the top.

~ 1 Hour Drive
